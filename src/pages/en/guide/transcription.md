# Transcription

## How it works

Engra uses an optimized AI transcription engine that runs entirely on your machine. No audio data is ever sent externally.

## Transcription modes

### Real-time
During recording, Engra transcribes **in 5-second segments**. You see text appearing live.

### Post-recording
After stopping, a complete transcription is generated from the final audio (better quality).

## Diarization

Engra identifies different speakers in the transcription. Speakers are displayed as **[Speaker 1]**, **[Speaker 2]**, etc.

## Supported languages

The engine supports over 90 languages. The most common:
- English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Portuguese
- Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic

Language is detected automatically or can be configured in settings.

## Choosing a model

The larger the model, the better the quality, but the more resources required. The **Standard** model offers the best balance for most users.
