# First recording

## Start a recording

1. Open Engra
2. Click the **Record** button or use the **Cmd+Shift+R** shortcut (macOS)
3. Choose audio sources:
   - **Microphone**: your voice
   - **System audio**: other participants' sound (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
4. Click **Start**

## Automatic detection

Engra automatically detects ongoing meetings:
- **Zoom**, **Teams**, **Google Meet**, **Slack Huddle**, **Discord**
- A banner appears to suggest recording

You can enable **auto-record** in settings to start recording automatically.

## During recording

- Transcription happens **in real time** (every 5 seconds)
- A live summary is generated if AI is active
- The menu bar icon turns red

## Stop recording

- Click **Stop** or use **Cmd+Shift+R**
- Engra automatically starts:
  1. Full transcription
  2. AI summary
  3. Entity extraction for the knowledge graph

## Meeting templates

Choose a template for better summaries:
- **General**: standard summary
- **Standup**: focus on blockers and progress
- **Retro**: what worked, what to improve, actions
- **Sales call**: client needs, objections, next steps
- **Interview**: skills, impressions, decision
- **Brainstorm**: generated ideas, votes, selection
