# Welcome to Engra

Engra is a desktop meeting recorder that works **100% locally** on your machine. It combines:

- **Audio recording** (microphone + system audio)
- **Automatic AI transcription** running locally
- **AI summaries** generated directly on your workstation
- **Knowledge graph** across meetings

No data is sent to any external server. Your recordings, transcriptions and summaries stay exclusively on your computer.

## Supported platforms

- **macOS** 13+ (Apple Silicon + Intel)
- **Linux** x64 (Ubuntu 22.04+, Fedora 38+, Arch)
- **Windows** 10/11 64-bit
