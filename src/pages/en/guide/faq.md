# FAQ

## General

**Is Engra really 100% local?**

Yes. The only network connections are:
- License verification — once a month
- Initial AI model download
- YouTube imports (optional)

No audio, transcription or summary is ever sent to a server.

**Do I need an internet connection?**

No, after the initial setup. Engra has a 30-day grace period for offline license validation.

**What languages are supported?**

The transcription engine supports 90+ languages. The Engra interface is available in French and English.

## Technical

**How much RAM do I need?**

- **8 GB**: sufficient for transcription + lightweight AI models
- **16 GB**: recommended for more advanced models

**How much disk space?**

- Engra: ~200 MB
- Transcription model: ~500 MB
- AI model for summaries: 1-8 GB depending on the level chosen
- Recordings: ~5 MB/minute (WAV)

**Can I use my own AI models?**

Yes. Any compatible model installed on your machine can be used in Engra.

## License

**Can I transfer my license?**

Yes. Deactivate a device in **Settings > License**, then activate on the new one.
